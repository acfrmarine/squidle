BENTHOZ-2015 Dataset

BENTHOZ-2015 has been published as an open data set with the Scientific Data journal of the Nature Publishing Group. Scientific Data is an open-access, peer-reviewed publication for descriptions of scientifically valuable datasets. The data descriptor that documents details and methods used to collect the data can be found here*, with instructions for citation. BENTHOZ-2015 is comprised of over 400,000 point labels from four Australian research groups mapped to more than 100 classes on the CATAMI classification scheme. The intention is to provide a standard data set for research in robotics, computer vision, and marine science. The data set is released under a creative commons license, with the intention of providing the raw data to researchers to maximise reuse.
