Welcome! Behind this popup window are a list of images. We need your help with tagging them! Here is what you need to do:

  1. Click a point on the image.
  2. Click a tag from the list on the right to assign the point to a class label. Make sure you assign the label for what is directly under the point you clicked.
  3. Move to the other points within the image until they are all labeled.
  4. Once you have labeled all the points in an image, click to go to the next image.
  5. Good luck, and have fun! The more images you label, the further you progress through the ranks and the more you are helping Science!

Click "Next" below to get started and to see a brief tutorial. Otherwise, if you are already a pro, simply click "Close" to continue without a tutorial.
